Reloved Teddies Cookie Policy – Transparency and Control

At Reloved Teddies, we place enormous importance on your privacy and clarity of communication. This cookie policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website, where we share the stories of our cherished bears and offer restoration services, while protecting your personal data in accordance with stringent UK (UK GDPR) and European privacy regulations 🇬🇧. We are committed to being transparent about every aspect of your visit, just as we are with every bear we restore.

How cookies work on our website

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our site. They help us remember your preferences (such as your favourite bear categories or your language choice), optimise our site performance, and ensure a smooth, personalised browsing experience. Without some essential cookies, features such as keeping items in your basket or remembering your login status for a wishlist would not work correctly. Think of them as tiny helpers that make your visit more enjoyable, much like a well-placed stitch on a beloved bear.

What data we collect and why?

We collect only the data necessary to provide you with a secure, stable, and heartwarming experience on our platform. This includes technical information about your device and browser, anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which bears are most viewed), and your preferences (such as notification settings for new arrivals). We use this data solely for legitimate, limited purposes, such as improving our content, understanding which restoration stories resonate most, and enhancing our services for bear lovers across the United Kingdom. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. Your trust is as precious to us as a vintage Chad Valley bear.

External service providers and tracking

We work with reputable external entities (such as analytics platforms like Google Analytics, and payment processors) that help us understand site traffic, process orders securely, and optimise our services. These partners are contractually bound to the same high standards of data protection that we apply to our own restoration work. Any tracking by third parties is conducted with respect for your privacy and in full compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR). We choose partners who share our values of care and integrity.

Your control over cookie preferences

You retain full control over your cookie preferences at any time. During your first visit to our site, you can use our cookie banner to choose which types of cookies (aside from strictly necessary ones) you wish to accept – just like choosing which bear to take home. You can later change your preferences at any time using your web browser settings or by checking the privacy settings in your account (if you have created one to follow a bear's journey). We believe in giving you the choice, every step of the way.

Updates and contact

This cookie policy may be periodically updated to meet new legal requirements or improved practices, much as we update our restoration techniques. Important changes will be communicated appropriately on our site, perhaps with a friendly notice. If you have any questions regarding this cookie policy – or if you just want to share a story about your own bear – please contact us via the channels below. We are always here to listen.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the basic functions and security of the site, like keeping your basket safe while you browse.
  • Preference cookies: Store your language, regional, and display settings, so you always see bears in a way that suits you.
  • Statistical/Analytical cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve content and feature the bears you love most.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to measure the relevance of our communications and show you information about new bears or restoration tips (only with opt-in consent).
  • Performance cookies: Optimise the loading speed and responsiveness of our pages, ensuring a smooth and cuddly browsing experience.

Your privacy rights and control

  • Accept or reject cookies during your first visit via the clear cookie banner.
  • Adjust your preferences at any time using your browser settings (look for the privacy section).
  • Enable or disable specific categories of cookies (e.g., marketing) through our preference centre.
  • Submit a request to access or correct your personal data – we are happy to help.
  • Object to automated processing or profiling; we do not engage in intrusive profiling.

At Reloved Teddies, we believe that transparency and control are the foundations of trust – just as they are in finding the perfect forever home for a rescued bear. This cookie policy is designed to give you full insight and control over your data, while offering the most heartwarming and user-friendly experience as you explore our collection of reloved teddies. By continuing to use our platform, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
