Reloved Teddies Privacy Policy – Transparency and Protection of Your Data

Last updated: 26 February 2026

At Reloved Teddies, we place great importance on protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you visit our website, interact with our content, or purchase our lovingly restored teddy bears and soft toys across the United Kingdom 🇬🇧. We are fully transparent about our data processing practices and operate in accordance with stringent UK privacy regulations, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What data we collect and how it improves your experience

To provide you with the best possible experience on our platform, whether you are browsing our collection of vintage teddy bears or making a purchase, we collect specific personal data. This data helps us improve our products, services, and content, and ensures you receive a safe, personalised, and seamless experience across Great Britain.

Personal data enabling identification

  • Name and email address (when you create an account, sign up for our newsletter, or request a bear restoration quote).
  • Contact details (including phone number and postal address) when submitting queries via our contact form or completing a purchase.
  • Account data enabling access to order history, wish lists, and personalised content.
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment partners – we never store full card details).

Technical and usage data

  • IP address and device information for security, fraud prevention, and to understand our audience better.
  • Browser type and version to optimally display our site and ensure performance across all devices.
  • Page views, click behaviour, and visited products to analyse interests and improve our offerings.
  • Interaction data with our website, including newsletter opens and link clicks.

All our data collection activities comply with UK 🇬🇧 privacy regulations and aim to improve your experience on the Reloved Teddies platform. We never collect more data than is strictly necessary for the services provided, and we are committed to minimising data collection wherever possible.

The role of cookies in personalising your experience

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to optimise your experience on Reloved Teddies. They play a key role in providing a secure and personalised browsing environment for our visitors across the United Kingdom, helping us remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our site.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for basic functions, security, and integrity of the website – such as keeping items in your basket while you shop.
  • Functional cookies: Store your preferences (language, region, recently viewed items) and settings.
  • Analytical cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site, which pages are most popular, and how we can improve our content and product range.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to measure the relevance of our communications and show you personalised offers (always with your explicit consent).

Managing cookies

You retain full control over cookie settings via your browser or our cookie banner, which appears when you first visit. Please note: disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of parts of the Reloved Teddies website, such as the shopping cart and checkout process. Our cookie settings are specifically tailored to the needs of users in Great Britain 🇬🇧 and comply with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR.

Your data is used only for legitimate purposes that improve your experience with Reloved Teddies and guarantee the security of our platform. We believe in complete transparency about how we process your information.

Rigorous data sharing protocols with trusted partners

Reloved Teddies shares your data only with carefully selected partners when necessary to provide our services (e.g., delivery, payment processing, hosting, or analytics). All partners must meet our rigorous privacy and security standards and are contractually bound as "processors" – they can only act on our documented instructions.

Categories of data recipients

  • Delivery and courier services: To ship your beloved teddies to your address across the UK.
  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal): For secure transaction handling – we never store your full financial data.
  • Hosting and IT service providers: For the technical operation, security, and maintenance of our website.
  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics): For anonymous analysis of site usage to optimise our content and product offerings.
  • Email service providers: For sending service emails (order confirmations) or marketing newsletters (if you have subscribed).
  • Legal authorities: Only when there is a clear legal obligation under UK law.

International data transfers

If data is transferred to countries outside the United Kingdom, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as UK International Data Transfer Agreements (IDTA) or Addendums. Our data processing always remains compliant with UK data protection law, regardless of where the data is processed. We prioritise partners based in the UK or within countries with adequacy regulations.

Your rights to access, correct, or delete your data

Under UK GDPR, you have extensive rights regarding your personal data. Reloved Teddies fully respects these rights and provides simple procedures to exercise them. We are committed to responding to all requests promptly and without undue delay.

Your privacy rights

  • Right of access: Insight into what data about you is being processed and a copy of that data.
  • Right to rectification: Correcting inaccurate or incomplete data about you.
  • Right to erasure: Deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten") under certain conditions, such as when data is no longer needed.
  • Right to restriction of processing: Temporary restriction of the processing of your data while a complaint is resolved.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest or for direct marketing purposes (including profiling).
  • Rights related to automated decision-making: Not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing.

How to exercise your rights

  1. If you have an account: log in and go to your account privacy settings to update or download your data.
  2. Submit a request via our online contact form clearly stating which right you wish to exercise.
  3. Contact our Data Protection Officer directly using the email address below.
  4. Clearly specify which right the request concerns and provide sufficient information to verify your identity (we may need to request additional ID).
  5. We will respond to your request within the statutory period of one month, extendable by two months for complex requests.

For questions regarding your privacy rights, you can contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@relovedteddies.co.uk. All requests are handled in accordance with UK data protection law, and you also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you are unsatisfied with our response.

Keeping you informed – updates to our privacy policy

We regularly review our privacy policy and may make changes to adapt to new legal requirements, improvements to our services, or changes in how we process data. We assure you that you will be informed of significant changes in a timely manner, because your trust is important to us.

Notification of changes

  • The 'Last updated' date at the top of this policy will be changed to reflect the most recent version.
  • Important changes will be communicated via a prominent banner on our website or by email (for account holders who have subscribed to updates).
  • The policy will always be available at the same link: /privacy-policy/.

Previous versions

Previous versions of our privacy policy will remain available upon request. We recommend periodically reviewing this policy to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. By continuing to use the Reloved Teddies platform after changes are made, you accept the updated policy. If you do not agree with the changes, you may close your account and cease using our services.
